## 🎯 Quick Steps to Upload Images

### Upload Locations:
1. **CEO Photo**: `public/images/leadership/h.png`
2. **Company Logo**: `public/images/logo/LOGO.png`
3. **About Section Image**: `public/images/semiconductors/abot.png`

### Steps:
1. **Navigate to your project folder**
2. **Go to the appropriate folder** (see locations above)
3. **Copy your image file** with the exact filename
4. **Refresh the website** - images will automatically appear!

## 🖼️ Image Display Info

### CEO Photo (h.png)
- **Shows in**: Leadership section
- **Style**: Square crop with decorative frame
- **Recommended**: 400x400px minimum, professional headshot

### Logo (LOGO.png)
- **Shows in**: Header, Footer
- **Style**: Auto-sized, transparent background recommended
- **Recommended**: 200x100px, PNG format

### About Image (abot.png)
- **Shows in**: About section
- **Style**: Full container width, video aspect ratio
- **Recommended**: 1200x800px, high quality
